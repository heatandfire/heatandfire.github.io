New grant from Faraday Institution- SAFEBATT

The Heat and Fire Lab is joining the Faraday Institution Major Project SAFEBATT! With a new project focussing on modelling thermal runaway and fire propagation in lithium-ion batteries, We hope that by including modelling fire dynamics aspects, to understand and predict the consequence of battery failure and the spike in heat output of flaming cells, fire science and fire engineering techniques can shed light on the thermal runaway so destructive to battery cells.

March 30, 2023.
