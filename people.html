Dr Alireza Sardmadian
Alireza joined the group as a Postdoctoral Research Associate in October 2021, studying the thermophysical behaviour of a LIB cell during its operation and lifetime as part of an EPSRC Prosperity Partnership with Jaguar Land Rover on vehicle electrification. He received his PhD in Mechanical Engineering in September 2021 from the University of Sussex, funded by the University of Sussex Chancellor's International Research Scholarship, and his thesis focused on heat transfer and control engineering. He received the best Paper award at both the 15th HEFAT Conference and FFHMT'21 Conference.
Hosein Sadeghi
Hosein joined the group as a PhD student in June 2022, studying multiphysics simulations of Lithium-ion battery fires. His background is in Mechanical Engineering, Thermofluids. Prior to joining KCL, he was a research assistant at the fire safety engineering group of Aalto University funded by Academy of Finland and the Finnish Fire Protection Fund. His research focused on radiative properties of the fuel vapors in fires and he graduated from Aalto University with his Licentiate degree in 2022. In his free time, Hosein enjoys playing Iranian traditional music. He shares some of his videos on his Youtube Channel
Imogen Richards
Imogen joined the group as a PhD student in October 2021, studying the fundamental dynamics causing fire spread mechanism for wood in various environmental and ambient conditions, including effects of moisture, wind and fuel type. Her background is in Mechanical Engineering, she carried out a MEng Engineering degree at the University of Cambridge in 2021.
